Meet Amanda

Building what I wished existed

"I saw how often women were handed fragmented, oversimplified advice about their bodies and lives and I knew we deserved something more honest."

Amanda Henry is a founding team member of Therabody and a wellness and brand leader with over 15 years of experience building culturally influential brands at the intersection of wellness, fashion, and technology.

She spent nearly a decade helping scale Therabody into a global brand, leading global communications, major partnerships, and women's wellness initiatives. Along the way, she saw both the power of the wellness industry and its gaps.

Well, Honestly is the platform she wished existed: nuanced, culturally aware, and rooted in the belief that women deserve better than trends, fragments, or one-size-fits-all advice.
